- LinkedIn Page
- LinkedIn Group
- Facebook Page
- Facebook Group
- Google Group (aimlcommunitybd@googlegroups.com)
- WhatsApp Channel

Find all of our social media accounts here: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KXw6NB8saYZYF-nH5YK5poqHuZF30urdWK1uOxBU8Es/edit?tab=t.0 

## How do we announce our upcoming activities?
1. Event team confirms event type and date with specific mentor (s)
2. Media team creates a google form for registration
3. The design team prepares a poster and/or thumbnail for the event.
4. The event team confirms the design and form admin and specific mentor.
5. The media team first announce the event on the following media respectively: 
    - Discord #announcement channel
    - LinkedIn Page 
    - LinkedIn Group
    - Facebook Page
    - Facebook Group
    - Send announcement email to aimlcommunitybd@googlegroups.com
    - WhatsApp Channel
6. The Media team creates Discord Events and LinkedIn Page events. 
7. On the day of the event, the Event team reminds specific mentor(s) and email registered participants with joining info. 

## What to write on the announcement post?
- Sentence(s) that creates hook and shows value
- Event title and schedule
- Name and Details of event lead and host
- Registration details
- Hashtags (if applicable)
- Photo such as poster/banner/thumbnail
